    Create Lesson Plans for History Classes

    Cater to the needs of the students. History is full of dates, names and locations. Essentially, a whole bunch of things to remember. Without the innate love for the History subject, it can be a pain studying and creating lesson materials for the teacher, and to go through such a class for a student. This is why History Plotter is here! This website allows you to store Historical information for materials, use these pieces of information, create connections between them, and create interactive lesson materials for the students. It even allows an automated quiz taken from a created lesson.
